FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before starting your free trial.
💰 Pricing & Billing
What does the free trial include?+
Your 30-day free trial includes 100% of the plan you choose — every feature, your dedicated advisor, bank linking, payroll, AI receipt scanning, and all automations. Your card is held but not charged until day 31. Cancel with 7 days notice before day 31 and you're never billed.
Can I switch plans later?+
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade mid-term, we prorate the difference. Downgrading takes effect at your next renewal date. Contact your advisor to make any plan changes.
Are there any hidden fees or per-employee charges?+
None. Unlike QuickBooks ($6.50–$12 per employee per month for payroll), IEBC charges one flat subscription fee regardless of how many employees you have. All features — payroll, bank linking, HR, reports, tax center — are included at every price tier.
What's the cancellation policy?+
Month-to-month: cancel with 7 days notice before your next billing date. Annual or 2-year: cancel with 7 days notice before your renewal date. A prorated refund is available on unused time for annual and 2-year plans. We never lock you in with hidden exit fees.
🏦 Bank Linking & Security
Can IEBC move or access my money?+
No. Bank linking uses read-only credentials — we can see and import your transactions, but we have zero ability to move, transfer, or touch your funds. This is enforced at the API level and is a hard technical limitation, not just a policy.
Which banks can I link?+
IEBC supports linking for all major U.S. banks including Chase, Wells Fargo, Bank of America, Citibank, US Bank, Capital One, TD Bank, PNC, Regions, and thousands of smaller banks and credit unions. You can also manually enter accounts or upload CSV statements for any bank not on the list.
Is my financial data encrypted?+
Yes. All data in transit is encrypted with 256-bit SSL/TLS. All stored data — transactions, payroll records, documents — is encrypted at rest using AES-256. We are PCI DSS compliant, SOC 2 Type II audited, and store your data on geo-redundant servers with daily backups.
💵 Payroll & HR
Is payroll really included — no add-on?+
Yes, full payroll is included in your flat subscription. This includes automated payroll runs, direct deposit, federal and state tax withholding, W-2 generation for employees, and 1099-NEC generation for contractors. There is no per-employee fee — ever.
How does direct deposit work?+
Payroll processes via ACH. Funds are deposited to employee accounts 1–2 business days before your specified pay date. You set the pay schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, semi-monthly, or monthly) and IEBC handles the rest automatically.
Can I handle both employees and 1099 contractors?+
Yes. IEBC tracks salaried and hourly employees (W-2) and independent contractors (1099-NEC) in the same system. At year-end, W-2s and 1099s are generated automatically based on payment history. The system alerts you when a contractor crosses the $600 filing threshold.
⚙️ Features & Setup
How long does setup take?+
Most clients are fully set up in under 30 minutes. Your advisor walks you through linking your bank accounts, importing historical data, and configuring payroll on your first onboarding call — which happens within 1 business day of signing up.
Can I migrate from QuickBooks or Zoho Books?+
Yes. IEBC imports QuickBooks .QBO and .IIF exports, Zoho Books CSV exports, FreshBooks CSV exports, and standard bank statement CSVs. Your advisor handles the migration for you — just export your data from the old system and share it with us.
What is the AI receipt scanning accuracy?+
IEBC's AI (powered by Claude) achieves 99%+ accuracy on standard receipts — vendor name, amount, date, and category. For ambiguous documents, the system flags the item for your review rather than auto-filing with an error. You can also set custom rules to improve accuracy for your specific vendors.
Is there a mobile app?+
Yes. The IEBC mobile app (iOS and Android) lets you snap receipts from your camera, check balances, approve payroll, and message your advisor. Employee self-service is also available on mobile for pay stubs, PTO requests, and onboarding documents.
